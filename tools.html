About these tools

These calculators use actual City of Round Rock utility rates effective November 1, 2025, including the five-tier water schedule and the winter average wastewater billing method. Round Rock bills residential sewer based on the average water consumption during November, December, January, and February. A leak running during that window inflates your winter average and raises your sewer charge for the following 12 months, a detail most generic leak calculators miss entirely.
